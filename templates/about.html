About Fraud Rescue

We are a professional service helping victims of online fraud navigate reporting procedures, documentation, and verification for law enforcement agencies.

Our Mission

Fraud Rescue is committed to supporting victims of online fraud, including: investment scams, romance scams, cryptocurrency schemes, impersonation attacks, and more. We help collect evidence, structure complaints, and guide reporting to legitimate authorities such as the FBI and INTERPOL.
