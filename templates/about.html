About Fraud Rescue

Fraud Rescue is an independent support service focused on assisting victims of online fraud. We help individuals understand what happened, organize their case information, and take the appropriate steps toward reporting scams through legitimate channels.

Online fraud continues to evolve globally, affecting individuals across financial, emotional, and digital spaces. Our goal is to provide structured, practical guidance to victims who may not know where to begin after experiencing a scam.

What We Do

We assist with reviewing case details, organizing evidence, and preparing structured reports that can be submitted to relevant organizations such as financial institutions, cybercrime units, and international law enforcement agencies where applicable.

What We Do Not Do

Fraud Rescue is not a law enforcement agency, government body, or financial institution. We do not have the authority to investigate crimes or recover funds, and we do not guarantee recovery outcomes. Our role is strictly supportive and informational.

Our Approach

  • Clear and structured case documentation
  • Evidence-based review process
  • Guidance on appropriate reporting channels
  • Transparent communication with clients
